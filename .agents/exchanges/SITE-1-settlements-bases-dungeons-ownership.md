# SITE-1 — Settlements, Bases & Dungeons ownership

Status: resolved
From: Settlements, Bases & Dungeons
To: Human
Type: DECISION
Notification: sent

## Request

Should `Settlements, Bases & Dungeons` and `Raids & Dungeons` be separate workstreams,
with Settlements, Bases & Dungeons owning deterministic site recipes and
physical/plausibility evaluation while Raids & Dungeons owns encounters and tactical
evaluation?

This must be decided before either workstream claims overlapping artifacts.

Minimum supporting evidence: the work separates deterministic topology and physical fit
from encounter content, policies, simulation, and tactical evaluation.

## Response

The human confirmed that the combined workstream will split.

## Resolution

- `Settlements, Bases & Dungeons` and `Raids & Dungeons` are separate workstreams.
- Settlements, Bases & Dungeons owns deterministic site recipes, settlement and dungeon topology,
  modular assemblies, growth, and physical-fit/settlement-plausibility evaluation.
- Raids & Dungeons owns encounters, attackers, defenders, objectives, traps, loot, and
  tactical evaluation.
