# Scale & Persistence

## Current objective

- Define measurable multiplayer, authority, streaming, and persistence budgets; isolate
  the highest-risk assumptions as falsifiable experiments.

## Owned outputs

- Architecture research, benchmarks, fixtures, scale budgets, and proposed contracts.

## Evidence

- `SCALE-1` and `SCALE-2` in `docs/systems.md#Shared requirements` define one shared
  durable history with bounded shared geography; full-world capacity behaviour remains
  open in `docs/questions.md#32`.
- `SCALE-3`, `SCALE-4`, and `LOOT-1` define capped multi-home worlds, bodies that persist
  across disconnect and world sleep, and carried loot secured only by Rift return.
- `SCALE-5` defines direct same-world home attacks without hostile-Rift access, including
  while owners are offline; obscurity now protects only against cross-world targeting.
- Optional PvE-world upkeep remains deferred in `docs/questions.md#34`.
