# Gates

The central object: every planet-base sits on a Gate, and all reach flows through
it. High-level here; the deep treatment is [[gate-physics|Gate Physics]] (src:
raw/design-overview.md).

## What a Gate is

A found, claimable wormhole linking two distant points. Gates **woke sealed** and
**cannot be manufactured** — only found, claimed, repaired (or, maybe, a stabilized
Rift). Their finiteness is the root of all conflict. [decided]

## How it works

See [[gate-physics|Gate Physics]] — the wormhole basis, why it needs fuel, stable
vs unstable (Gate vs Rift), time, and what passes through.

## In the game
