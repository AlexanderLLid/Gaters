# Taming

_Stub — narrow scope decided (TAME-1), specifics open. See raw/design-overview.md → Taming, and [[rifts|Rifts]]._

- **Narrow yes:** include **taming**; decline deep ARK-style breeding/genetics (a babysitting chore by construction — the exact pain Gaters is built to remove; see [[building|Building]]).
- **Source:** creatures come **only from [[rifts|Rifts]]** — a monster-spill rolls a `tameable` tag; Rift rank scales rarity. No new spawn system.
- **Taming is a Rift-clear objective, not a feed-and-wait timer** — subdue under the [[mask-energy|away-reserve]] leash; an event, not a maintenance state.
- **Lineage is a home-anchored record** (safe while [[coordinates|unfound]]); the living animal is the **exposed wager** (costs a core; can die and be looted; the registry entry survives).
- **"Breeding" is shallow and lossless** — combine two registry entries for a blended/mutated re-roll; no timer.
