# World Types

The place-types of Gaters, kept high-level. They double as the unlock ladder (see [[progression|Progression]]). [tentative]

## The types

- **Home planet-base** — your claimed world on a single [[Gates|Gate]]; the seat of building and solo PvE. Safe **while unfound**, not merely while sealed — see [[coordinates|Coordinates & Obscurity]].
- **Surface worlds** — the starting tier (only short-range Gates have woken); larger and richer worlds as you progress. Each world's seed rolls a **biome tag set**, so one world's output is another's missing input — the base trade driver (REGION-1).
- **Hub worlds** — neutral, **lawless commerce crossroads**: trade routing + tolls, **capturable for tax control**; the open PvP contest lives on contested frontier worlds, not here. See [[hub-worlds|Hub Worlds]].
- **The Safe Core** — the [[united-gate-coalition|United Gate Coalition]]-policed market and new-player spawn; safe commerce, always reachable.
- **The Frontier** — the unclaimed worlds you reach by **dialing out** ([[Gates|Gate]], FRONTIER-1); the PvE/PvPvE contact surface where "reach equals exposure" rules. A frontier world is a database row until visited, a **solo PvE** tick when you're there alone, a **contested** instance when others arrive (see [[Gates]]). Its **biome tag set** (REGION-1, below) plus monster-spill ([[taming|Taming]], TAME-1) give it its flavour; claim its Gate to make it yours.
